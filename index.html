CLASS

Dragon Slayer

Dragon Slayer possesses powerful sword skills. Has limited surviving skills but high defense parameter than other class. Lacks ranged skills. Their stylish attack and ultimates will devastate enemies. Well known for killing dragons.

Dark Wizard

Dark Wizard possesses powerful magic skills. Can damage multiple enemies using their dark magic. Has lowest health but highest mana which enables them to cast catastrophic magic skills. Lacks close combat skills.

Shadow Priest

Shadow Priest possesses highly effective healing spells that can save the ones around shadow priest. And yet they are capable of casting shadow spells that can devastate enemies but it is not effective as Dark Wizard's dark magic.

GAME PLAY

Dynamic Combat

Combat system is so Dynamic. The flow of the combat will not give you time to be bored! You will have to focus not to die in the field !

Co-Op play

You can play with friends. Connect with your buddies and conquer the Fantasy world ! It makes it more fun!

Fun Boss Fights

There will be many boss fights in every dungeon and field maps. Conquer them so that you can grab nice weapon and gears!
